
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git stores my repository on a server, allowing me to access my work if it was deleted on my system.
Furthermore, Git records changes that I make to a repository and will allow me to quickly undo if there was a mistake.
I am also able to share progress and collaborate with others through Git, making it essential when working in a team.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: A field that data science could be applied to is business.
For example, a company with a lot of customer records can use data science to
analyze their data. They can identify purchase patterns and create targeted
marketing campaigns to improve their sales through the use of data science.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging brings two branches together and preserves the history of the project.
On the other hand, rebase effectively rewrites the commit history of the source branch.
By eliminating these merge commits, it can result in a cleaner commit history.
